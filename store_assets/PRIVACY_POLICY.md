# Privacy Policy for SchoolBell

**Last Updated: 2024-07-10**

DolphinWing ("we," "our," or "us") operates the **SchoolBell** mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**SchoolBell is designed with privacy in mind.** 
- We **DO NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII).
- All schedule data, labels, and settings are stored **locally** on your device using Android's Room database and DataStore.
- No data is uploaded to any external servers.

## 2. Permissions Required
To provide its core functionality, the app requests the following permissions:
- **Notifications**: To display the ringing alert and controls.
- **Exact Alarms**: To ensure the bell rings precisely at the scheduled time.
- **Media Playback**: To play the chosen bell sound in the background.

These permissions are used strictly for the app's operation and are not used to collect any user data.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect information used to identify you.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository: [https://github.com/DolphinWing/SchoolBell](https://github.com/DolphinWing/SchoolBell)
